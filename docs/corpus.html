Paramore Through the Ages Corpus Analysis

This project aims to analyze the lyrical patterns across multiple generations of albums from the pop-rock group Paramore. Here, I've gathered the text files of Paramore's most popular albums from different eras in their career; this analysis will be broken up into early Paramore, mid Paramore, new Paramore, and one of Hayley William's independent albums.
